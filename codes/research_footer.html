Canon is a multinational organization headquartered in Ohta-Ku, Tokyo that typically specializes in various kinds and varieties of optical, imaging and several related industrial products including different version and types of printers, scanners and also various categories of medical equipment and their pivotal products i.e. lenses, cameras and related technological devices along with diverse equipment used in the manufacturing of semiconductors.They were established in August 10 ,1937
