ASUS

ASUS, being a multinational, globally recognized and renowned computer, phone hardware and electronics manufacturer based on Taiwan has grossing sales unit all over the globe. Specialized in the sector of gaming and performance, they manufacture technological hardware that are on par with any other cutting-edge competitors around the globe. They were established in 1989.They are the world`s No.1 motherboard and gaming brand driven by innovation and quality.


We were inspired by the smooth , dynamic , user- friendly and interactive navigation bar from ASUS and recognized it to be our inspiration. The ASUS navigation bar is divided into eight sections, Mobile/Handhelds , Laptops ,Displays/Desktops ,Motherboards/Components ,Networking/IoT/Servers ,Accessories , Support and search and login(counted as a single section) with tons of other features and components making it attractive and also operatively excellent.Their navigation bar has two partition, with the main partition being the lower one and the upper partitiond displaying their sub brands


What is Similar?

Our entire navigation bar is also divided into two partitions, upper and lower.

We have similar logo on the left side of the upper partition like the Asus website and also a small section about our store and customer service in the very right of the same partition.

We have a search and login section in the very right of the bottom partition.

We navigate smoothly to different pages.


What is Different?

On Hovering our bar shows an underline & clicking on the various sections of our navigation bar it directly takes us to the respective page but on hovering the reference website a new sub partition appears with new sub sections and by clicking on those desired section we navigate to their respective page.

Their sub section also contains images on the right that link to those products, but we have no such feature.

The search section in our bar is an input type label with no new sections, but the search section on ASUS is a logo and on clicking comes another new sub section entirely for the search bar.
