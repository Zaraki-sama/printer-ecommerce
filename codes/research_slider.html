In every e-commerce website slider plays a pivotal role. It is generally added in the homepage to enhance the visual appeal of the website and catch the consumer`s eye and to highlight the major component of the website and deliver our say and principles to the consumers in an easy way and with our slider we have done just that. The automatic slider with high-quality printer image on the right with interesting message on the left helps us to maintain space efficiency in the site. Hewlett-Packard

Hewlett-Packard is an American multinational information technology company that mainly retails various kinds of hardware components and software, with appropriate and based services to its consumers. They are headquartered in Palo Alto, California and were newly emerged after the split of their parent company in 2015.They primarily develop various categories of Personal Computers (PCs), printers ,3D printing solutions. They have a short abbreviation which makes them popular worldwide and it`s the one and only “hp”. Hewlett-Packard being selling printers also makes it a rival of Only Prints!


Hewlett-Packer also consists of an automatic slider but with longer interval timer. The slider consists images on the right and a text message on the left. They also contain two arrow buttons on both side of the slider to manually change the slider. A small learn button is below the text that redirects to their own respective pages.The words in the slider seem to be divided into two partition by horizontal rule.

What is Similar?

Both the sliders contain images making the page visually attracting.

The slider in our as well as in the reference site change images automatically after a certain interval.

In both the sites, the sliders help in maintaining space efficiency.

The sliders in Only Prints and hp contain content highlighting text to highlight their content effectively.


What is Different?

The content highlighting text in Only Prints is not part of the slider whereas in the hp site it is.

We cannot change the images of the slider manually in our website however it can be done in the reference site.

The Shop now button is also not part of the slider whereas the Learn button of the reference site is the slider`s part.

The newly appeared Learn button redirects to a completely different page everytime but our button only redirects to Products page.
