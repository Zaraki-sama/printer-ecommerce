Print Friendly

With the primary motive and dedicated goal of simplifying the method of clearly printing various kinds of online files, documents, essentially diverse online contents, Print Friendly was launched in 2009.The main function of this organization is to convert web pages to PDFs, one of the most popular file formats out there. Even our coursework for the project is to be documented in the format of a PDF. There are various range of services offered by the company like not only converting but compressing PDFs, signing PDFs online, PDF readers, editing PDFs, converting them into JPG format, excel, word and vice-versa.


The smooth and mesmerizing style of the cards used in Printfriendly`s website inspired us to make our own. The cards are perfectly paced with no slow or fast feature . The cards contain logos along with a bit of text information and also have border displaying upon hovering.The cards return back to their original position in the same pappce as they transform when hovered.


What is Similar?

Both cards transform on hovering.

Both cards have shadow.
